Treatment

Cholera is a dangerous disease that can lead to rapid dehydration that can lead to death if not treated the treatment for this is called ORT oral rehydration therapy The idea of it is to replace lost fluids in the body and it comes in small packets its called ORS oral rehydration salts and its meant to be dissolved in  1 liter of safe water it can also be made by dissolving  6 level table spoons of sugar and half and half a small spoon of salt in 1 liter of preferably safe water.
