Affected Places

 The V Cholerae is found in water or food contaminated by feces or vomit meaning if you have a good supply of clean drinking water you wont not get cholera countries with low population with a clean supply of drinking water a low GNI and densely inhabited areas with unhygienic conditions that’s where the vibrio cholerae bacterium will be abundant and where the cholera disease will also be abundant.
