What Is Cholera

an infectious and often fatal bacterial disease of the small intestine,

its caused by a bacteria colled vibrio cholerae.

typically contracted from infected water supplies and causing severe vomiting

and diarrhoea, it causes the infected person to dehydrate to the point of death its one of the most rapidly fatal infectious illnesses known.
